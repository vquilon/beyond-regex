Privacy Policy

Your Privacy

Your privacy is extremely important to us. We use the information we collect on the site in order to improve Debuggex. We do not sell, trade, etc., any of the data we collect about you to any third party without your express permission. Safeguarding your privacy is our priority.

What we Gather

Debuggex uses Google Analytics to gather anonymous usage statistics in order to monitor the health of the site. We strongly encourage you to review their privacy policy.

Debuggex uses Mozilla Persona to deal with authentication. Persona is a kick-ass technology built to make authentication easier, safer, and privacy-aware. When you create an account, Debuggex does not store any information at all about your password. We store only your email; Persona handles the rest. We strongly encourage you to review their privacy policy.

When you share or save expressions, Debuggex stores everything about that expression. Anybody with access to the link will be able to view the expression that you saved. If you update an expression, Debuggex stores all previous versions of the expression as well, in order to enable you to view how an expression changed over time.
